# Clearance Overview (Summary Only)

This folder provides a high-level overview of security clearances and
government-related authorizations relevant to certain historical or
ongoing work.

Purpose:
- Acknowledge existence of clearance-governed activities
- Provide confidence to investors and advisors
- Avoid disclosure of classified or restricted information

Notes:
- No classified materials are stored in this data room
- Clearance identifiers, dates, and sponsoring entities may be
  summarized at a high level only
- Detailed records may be made available under appropriate clearance,
  NDA, or government process if required

Suggested contents:
- Clearance_Summary_<YYYY-MM-DD>.md
- Verification_Process_Notes_<YYYY-MM-DD>.md
