# NEW_DEVELOPER.md — Day 1 to Day 3 Onboarding (Read in Order)

This repo is intentionally structured to be readable by:
- future maintainers,
- assigned programmers,
- auditors / due diligence reviewers.

**Authoritative navigation:** start at `MASTER_INDEX.md`.

---

## Day 1 — Orientation + Rules of the Road

1) **README.md**  
   Goal: Understand what this repo is, what it is *not*, and where to start.

2) **MASTER_INDEX.md** (authoritative)  
   Goal: Learn the documentation map and where each topic lives.

3) **docs/DOCUMENTATION_ARCHITECTURE.md**  
   Goal: Understand document zones, governance boundaries, and indexing rules.

4) **SOP.md**  
   Goal: Learn operating discipline (including documentation placement rules).

5) **SECURITY.md**  
   Goal: Understand IP protection, access protocols, and safe handling expectations.

---

## Day 2 — Governance, Compliance, Audit Readiness

6) **AUDITOR_README.md**  
   Goal: Learn how audit evidence is structured and how to navigate it.

7) **docs/GOVERNANCE_EMERGENCY_MODE.md**  
   Goal: Understand solo-founder emergency governance and controlled merges.

8) **docs/GOVERNANCE_UNBROKEN_CHAIN.md**  
   Goal: Understand chain-of-custody and traceability expectations.

9) **docs/Governance/Snapshots/README.md**  
   Goal: How snapshots are created/used and what not to casually edit.

---

## Day 3 — Data Room & Domain-Specific Zones

10) **docs/Data_Room/README.md**  
    Goal: Understand the investor/diligence subset and what belongs there.

11) **docs/Data_Room/INVESTOR_DATA_ROOM_INDEX.md**  
    Goal: Follow the curated diligence package.

12) Zone scans (skim to learn where things go):
- Legal: `docs/01_Legal/*`
- Finance: `docs/02_Finance/*`
- Technical: `docs/03_Technical/*`
- Defense: `docs/04_Defense/*`

---

## Expectations for Contributions

- If you add a durable document, **link it in `MASTER_INDEX.md`**.
- Follow `SOP.md` documentation placement rules.
- Do not create new “index” files without explicit instruction.
- Treat Governance/Snapshots as controlled artifacts.
