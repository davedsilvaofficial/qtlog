# Purpose & Governance Context (IPO / Audit Summary)

This section provides a high-level, non-technical interpretation of each Quantum Trek (QT) working directory.  
It is intended for **IPO readiness, audit review, and governance clarity**, not for operational detail.

## qtlog_repo
**Purpose:**  
Primary system-of-record repository for governance, compliance, logs, SOPs, and automation related to Quantum Trek operations.

**Governance Role:**  
- Canonical audit trail for decisions, incidents, and emergency governance modes  
- Houses documentation required for due diligence, investor review, and regulatory posture  
- Suitable for read-only auditor access

**Risk Classification:** Medium–High (governance-critical, low code execution risk)

---

## qtlog_archives
**Purpose:**  
Long-term retention of historical logs, deprecated governance artifacts, and prior-period records.

**Governance Role:**  
- Supports evidentiary requirements (immutability, historical traceability)  
- Reduces operational clutter in active repositories  
- Preserves continuity for audits spanning multiple years

**Risk Classification:** Low (archive-only, non-operational)

---

## qt-notion
**Purpose:**  
Bridge layer between Notion-based operational planning (WBS, logs, investor notes) and the GitHub-backed governance record.

**Governance Role:**  
- Source material feeding formal records  
- Not authoritative until material is promoted into qtlog_repo  
- Supports human workflow without compromising audit integrity

**Risk Classification:** Medium (human-generated content, not system-of-record)

---

## qt
**Purpose:**  
Active working directory for experiments, prototypes, scripts, and non-governance development work.

**Governance Role:**  
- Explicitly **not** the system-of-record  
- Contents are promotable into qtlog_repo only after review  
- Allows rapid iteration without contaminating governance artifacts

**Risk Classification:** Variable (depends on contents; treated as non-authoritative)

Only **qtlog_repo** is considered an authoritative governance repository for:
- IPO due diligence
- Audit evidence
- Security and access control review
- Emergency governance actions

Other directories are **supporting or preparatory** in nature and are intentionally segregated to enforce clean governance boundaries.
