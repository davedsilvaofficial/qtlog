## Objective
To position the Quantum Trek Arc Reactor as a critical infrastructure asset for the Canadian Department of National Defence (DND) and the U.S. DoD (Defense Innovation Unit).

## Primary Use Cases (Dual-Use)
1. **Tactical Power Resiliency:** Mobile, high-output power for forward operating bases (FOBs) and remote northern deployments.
2. **Propulsion Integration:** Future-state QKF propulsion for heavy-lift aerial or maritime assets.
3. **Emergency Response:** Rapid deployment of power for DND-led disaster relief operations.

## Security & Compliance
* **Controlled Goods Program (CGP):** Quantum Trek is prepared to register for the CGP (Canada) to handle sensitive technical data.
* **ITAR/EAR Readiness:** Initial engineering is being logged with International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) boundaries in mind (see SECURITY.md).

## Milestone Roadmap
* **Milestone A:** Successful "Kumar Experiment" replication and data logging.
* **Milestone B:** Prototype submission to "IDEaS" (Innovation for Defence Excellence and Security) program.
* **Milestone C:** Deployment of Alpha unit for field testing in remote Canadian climates.
